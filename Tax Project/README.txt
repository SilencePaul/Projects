1. This program is able to calculate the tax return for year 2021 for all provinces except Quebec.
2. You can enter your personal information, T4 information, Tuition, CWB, Donations and Gift in the Tax_information.txt or Tax_information.xlsx
3. If you enter the wrong province name or wrong Birth date or wrong length of Sin number there will be an error occured.
4. For the T4, Tuition, CWB, Donations and Gifts part, please dont leave any blank. If you dont have the information, please enter 0 instead.
5. After press 'Run' button, you can choose to get information from txt or excel.
6. Then, the program will automaticly generate your tax refund or balance owing.
